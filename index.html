Scent of the Day

A perfume is a fragrance product that is applied to the skin or clothing to give off a pleasant odor. Perfumes are made from a mixture of essential oils, alcohol, and water.
The scents can be floral, spicy, fresh, woody, or oriental, and the concentration of fragrance can vary from eau de cologne (light) to parfum (strong).
